ABOUT PURE SPECTRUM 

Let's face it, the CBD industry has had its fair share of issues. Here at Pure Spectrum we aim to tackle two of the most important issues within this space: Legitimacy and quality. In order to accomplish this we need to start at the beginning from the source. That is why Pure Spectrum has entered into a one of kind, exclusive partnership with Colorado Cultivars. On top of being one of the largest, if not the largest, outdoor and indoor hemp farms in the nation, Colorado Cultivars is also registered with the Department of Agriculture in Colorado so that you don't have to worry about any fly by night operations.

Besides be registered with the Department of Agriculture, Colorado Cultivars is also organically certified. This means that your product will be clean of pesticides, herbicides, and heavy metals from the very beginning stages. Not only can you count on a clean product from the get go, but you will also have peace of mind knowing that everything from planting the seeds in the ground to the processing of the oil is being done through organic processes.
